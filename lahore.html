Badshahi Mosque

The Badshahi Mosque was built in 1673 by Emperor Aurangzeb. It represents the grandeur of Mughal architecture and is one of Lahore’s most iconic landmarks.

Lahore Fort

The Lahore Fort contains palaces, halls, and gardens built by different Mughal emperors over centuries. It is a symbol of Lahore’s rich cultural heritage.

Shalimar Gardens

Shalimar Gardens were built by Emperor Shah Jahan in 1641. The gardens represent peace, beauty, and symmetry.
